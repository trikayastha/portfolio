Hi! I’m Tribesh Kayastha.

I’m a product & business analyst. For the last four years I’ve worked on homegrown fintech, logistics, and ERP products built for the Nepali market — the kind of software real businesses run their day on.

My favourite part of the job is the messy middle: sitting with users, untangling what they actually need from what they say they want, and turning a thousand scattered feedback points into a roadmap someone can build. I like numbers that tell a story — retention curves, funnels, unit economics — and I like shipping the small nudges that move them.

Most recently I was a business analyst at Yirifi, a crypto and digital asset intelligence platform, where I built the analytics framework, freemium growth model, content strategy, and an AI-powered content pipeline that produced 20+ assets a week. Before that I spent a year and a half as lead product analyst at Karobar, an accounting platform used by 250k+ small businesses. When I’m away from the dashboards you’ll find me deep in Ironman training or building odd little web experiments for fun.
