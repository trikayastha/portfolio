## Intro

I'm a **product & business analyst**. For the last six years I've worked on homegrown fintech, logistics, crypto intelligence, and ERP products — the kind of software real businesses run their day on.

My favourite part of the job is the messy middle: sitting with users, untangling what they actually need from what they say they want, and turning a thousand scattered feedback points into a roadmap someone can build. I like numbers that tell a story — retention curves, funnels, unit economics — and I like shipping the small nudges that move them.

Most recently I was a **business analyst at Yirifi**, a crypto and digital asset intelligence platform, where I built the analytics framework, freemium growth model, content strategy, and a multi-agent AI pipeline that produced 20+ assets a week. Before that I spent a year and a half as **lead product analyst at Karobar**, an accounting platform used by 500K+ small businesses — where I ran market expansion into Indonesia, Nigeria, and the Philippines on top of the core analytics and retention work. When I'm away from the dashboards you'll find me deep in Ironman training or building odd little web experiments for fun.

**Crypto & digital asset intelligence**

Joined pre-launch to build the foundation: analytics framework across distinct user tiers, a freemium growth loop routing users from content discovery into gated platform features, and a full marketing website redesign separating it from the web app. Defined the B2C vs. B2B segmentation strategy and content framework, then backed it with a multi-agent AI pipeline that shipped 20+ assets a week — long-form blogs, LinkedIn series, daily market updates, vendor spotlights — without adding headcount.

### Karobar — 2024–2025
**Mobile & web accounting**
LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/company/karobar-app

Led product analytics through a full platform revamp for 500K+ users. Ran a 50-company closed beta — aggregating 1,200+ feedback points from calls, store reviews, and support tickets into a weighted backlog. End-to-end Mixpanel instrumentation moved D30 retention from 38% to 54% and grew DAU by 30%. Also led the international expansion push: researched and launched the app in Indonesia, Nigeria, and the Philippines, growing to 10K users in 3 months, and built the roadmap for broader SEA and African rollout.

Ran deep-dive discovery with 12 enterprise wholesale distributors — floor walks, stakeholder interviews, shadow sessions — then turned those workflows into BRDs, PRDs, and acceptance criteria tight enough for developers to ship bespoke ERP modules without constant back-and-forth.

### Upaya City Cargo — 2021–2022
**Logistics super-app**
LinkedIn: https://np.linkedin.com/company/upayaofficial

Built live Grafana dashboards that kept on-time delivery above 90% and idle rider time under 12%, and led enterprise integrations with Foodmandu and eSewa that drove 60%+ of order volume.
